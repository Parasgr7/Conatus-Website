RISE OF ULTRON

This game was based in the idea that the world is witnessing an outrageous situation of cold war; countries secretly trying to wage cyber wars against each other. In order to stop this, the UN’s artificial intelligence unit has installed an AI on the Internet. Upon activation, Ultron immediately perceived the human race as a “security threat”, and formulated a plan to systematically take control over humanity. Hence initiating unprecedented cyber-attacks by deploying viruses over the internet. Therefore, the UN has devised a Resistance Force to combat with the Ultron. All the teams were part of this resistance so they had to trace the ultron, demobilize it, and finally aim to breach its digital fortress.

COUNTER-STRIKE

This game was solely intended to engage players in virtual warfare where they associate themselves to either of the clan viz. the terrorist, the counter terrorist or the spectator on self volition. The mere aim of this game is to launch attacks and assaults on their adversaries, deploying spectrum of available weapons, wreaking havoc on maps and knock the opponent out. Game Version: Counter-Strike 1.6.

CODE-COSMONAUTS

A complete coding game as based on the idea that planet earth is deteriorating and there is no place left for human race to survive. So ,in order to save humanity ,the participants had to code and strengthen the building force which would support them in their struggle for survival against all odds .The participants had to participate in teams of 2.The contest was held in a single round and consisted of some problem statements with strict constraints of time.

B-TECH TROLLED

This game was solely based on the four years of B.tech that every engineer goes through .In this event they were given a chance to travel through .the time, through the ups and downs of the four years of their lives, facing the challenges during these groovy years. Participants were provided with the opportunity to take a walk down the lane and that too with a twist .They had to travel through each year and that too fighting with the time in a heart pumping event to remain in competition. This was the time to walk through the four crucial years of their lives in a few moments which surely pumped up their adrenaline rush.

THE LAST STRIKE

Team CONATUS conducted the event "THE LAST STRIKE" as the mid semester event of the society. The event was conducted in two rounds : the pre round was held on 18th Feb 2015,Wednesday in the back block during the lunch hours(12:40 p.m. to 2:20 p.m.) and the final round of the event was held on 23rd Feb 2015,Monday in the BB shed during the lunch hours. A total of around 460 teams participated in the pre round out of which 16 teams qualified to the final round.

FEB 18, 2015

EIGHTH HORCRUX

Team Conatus organized team event “THE EIGHTH HORCRUX” on November 14, 2013 which consisted of two rounds. Each team consisted of two participants. In the 1st round the participants were tested on various parameters such as C Language, Aptitude, Sports and Movies. The first round itself saw a record participation of about 400 teams and around 800 students from I, II, III and IV year. The event witnessed record participation of 800 students; this being the maximum participation in any society event till date. The second round, which was the final round of the event, was participated by 12 teams. The teams were judged on C and Harry Potter questions. The top 3 teams were awarded with cash prizes and certificates.
